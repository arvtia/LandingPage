Frequently Asked Questions

What is Couples Connect?

Couples Connect is a private space for two people to share memories, messages, and milestones. Whether you're side by side or long-distance, it helps you stay emotionally close and organized.

Is Couples Connect free to use?

Yes — the core experience is free. We’ll introduce optional upgrades later for those who want more customization or storage.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. Everything is encrypted and stored securely. Only you and your partner can access your shared space — no ads, no tracking.

Can we use it if we’re in a long-distance relationship?

Yes! Couples Connect is perfect for long-distance couples. You can share updates, send love notes, and feel close even when you're far apart.

How do I invite my partner?

When you sign up, you’ll get a unique partner code. Share it with your partner, and once they enter it, you’ll both be connected in your shared space.
